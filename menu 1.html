A rich and flavorful bowl Chicken that will leave you wanting more! This recipe is perfect for those who love bold, spicy flavors with a hint of warmth from the broth. Here's how to make it:

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Chicken broth
  • Spices (chili, garlic, ginger, etc.)
  • tomatoes
  • Vegetables (green onions, spinach)

Optional Toppings:

  • Soft-boiled egg
  • Nori (seaweed)
  • Sesame seeds
  • Chili oil
  • Fresh herbs (cilantro, basil)

Cooking Time:

Approximately 30 minutes

Servings:

Serves 2-4 people

Cooking steps

Follow these steps to create a delicious bowl of spicy ramen:

Preparation Time:

10 minutes

Cooking Time:

20 minutes

Total Time:

30 minutes

Servings:

2-4 servings

Cooking Steps:

  1. In a large pot, heat some oil over medium heat. Add the spices and saut√© until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Add the vegetables and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with your choice of toppings.
  6. Enjoy your delicious spicy ramen!
